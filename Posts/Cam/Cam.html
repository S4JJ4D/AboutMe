Saying Hi!

The input to the problem of cam profile design is the follower displacement profile which must be specified by the user. It should be expressed either as a function of cam angle \(\theta\) or as a function of time \(t\). A mapping is then constructed to relate \(t\) to \(\theta \). Design parameters include roller radius \(r_f\), base circle radius \(r_b \), and, offset \(e\).

Waldron and Wilson are good recourses to learn about cams.
